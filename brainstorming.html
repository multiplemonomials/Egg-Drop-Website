Brainstorming

Shack with Supporting Structure

(Jamie and Drew)

We decided to create a rectangular prism of solid toothpicks to hold the egg and protect it from shock. We also added triangular pyramids and rectangular prisms (we couldn't decide which) on the sides to absorb impact and channel it to the corners of the center prism.

Spike Shock Absorbers

(Hunter)

Hunter decided to create a small frame around the egg with many long (>10 cm) spikes protruding out. He theorized that those would flex and break during landing, and break his device's fall.
